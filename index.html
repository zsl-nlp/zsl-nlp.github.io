I am a tenure-track Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science at Tianjin University, and a member of TJUNLP Lab, led by Prof. Deyi Xiong. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academic of Science. My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Multimodal Processing, Machine Translation. I am looking for self-motivated students interested in building systems for large language model and deep neural network. Feel free to zhushaolin@tju.edu.cn if you want to join us!
